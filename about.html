About The Pre School

Kuda Kusum Pre School

The First Step Towards a Bright Future

Located in the peaceful village of Hingurana, Kuda Kusum Pre School is a nurturing learning space dedicated to shaping the early childhood of little ones. As a trusted preschool in the Eastern Province, it provides a strong foundation for children to begin their educational journey with confidence and joy. Known for its caring teachers, engaging activities, and child-friendly environment, Kuda Kusum Pre School focuses on developing creativity, social skills, and basic academics. With a perfect blend of learning and play, the school ensures that every child blossoms beautifully, ready to step into the future.

Our History

School Identity Kuda Kusum Pre School has a proud history of providing quality early education since its beginning.


The Beginning of Kuda Kusum Pre School

  • Pre School Name: Kuda Kusum Pre School
  • Started on: 2003.01.06
  • Initial Address: No. 10, 1/A, Wawa Para, Hingurana, Ampara
  • Founder Teacher: Shanthi Rathnayake
  • Number of Students at the Start: 3

The preschool later moved to a better location with improved facilities.

  • Relocated on: 2021.12.10
  • Current Address: Kuda Kusum Pre School, Market Place, Hingurana, Ampara
  • New Building Support: A fully equipped preschool building was constructed with the support of Hemas Outreach Foundation
